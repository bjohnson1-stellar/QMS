<objective>
## Goal
Integrate 452 schedule extraction entries into the equipment registry — add schedule_extractions as a 12th data source in the reconciler, enrich existing equipment_instances with schedule-authoritative attributes (manufacturer, model, HP, voltage, weight), create new instances for schedule-only equipment, and re-run conflict detection with the enriched data.

## Purpose
Phase 25 extracted 452 equipment entries from 19 MEP schedule sheets with rich attribute data (manufacturers, models, HP, voltage, weight, CFM) that the original drawing-based extraction mostly lacked. The reconciler currently reads 11 discipline-specific tables but skips schedule_extractions entirely. Until reconciled, the schedule data is invisible to conflict detection, spec compliance, the equipment dashboard, and future submittal cross-referencing.

Schedule data is the highest-confidence source for equipment specifications — it's the engineer's design intent. Enriching equipment_instances with this data makes every downstream feature more accurate.

## Output
- `pipeline/reconciler.py` updated to scan schedule_extractions as 12th source
- Existing equipment_instances enriched with manufacturer, model, HP, voltage, weight from schedules
- New equipment_instances created for schedule-only equipment (~100-200 estimated)
- Conflict detection re-run with enriched data
- Equipment dashboard reflects complete picture
</objective>

    ### B. Add schedule_extractions to _ATTR_COLUMNS

    Add entry for schedule_extractions with its column mappings so the generic scanner can understand the attribute structure.     ### C. Update _infer_type_name() for schedule equipment types

    ### D. Handle confidence-weighted attribute merging
